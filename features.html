Features

GPS Bus Tracking

With Busit you can have a live view of exactly where your desired bus is at all times.
Busit also provides percise ETA times that relies on GPS tracking so you
can never miss the bus, online or offline.

Never Miss Your Stop

When approaching your destination Busit will give an alert with a popup
window and sound notification to inform you that your stop is approaching,
saving time and unnecessary stress.
